Our Process

What is Hive-Made?

Every Rad Wax product starts in the hive. Our beeswax is ethically sourced from small-scale beekeepers who prioritize the health of their colonies above all else.

We blend that pure beeswax with minimal, pronounceable ingredients — coconut oil, shea butter, zinc oxide, and botanical extracts. Nothing synthetic. Nothing you can't find in nature.

The result is a line of wellness products that actually work, feel incredible on your skin, and leave zero trace on the planet. That's what hive-made means to us.
