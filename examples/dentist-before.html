Welcome to Pacific Dental Group

Welcome to Pacific Dental Group! We are a family-owned dental practice serving the La Jolla and greater San Diego community for over 15 years. Our team of experienced dentists and hygienists provide comprehensive dental care in a comfortable, modern office environment. We offer a full range of services including preventive care, cosmetic dentistry, restorative procedures, and emergency dental services.

At Pacific Dental Group, we believe that every patient deserves personalized attention and the highest quality care. Whether you need a routine cleaning or a complete smile makeover, our dedicated team is here to help you achieve your dental health goals. We use the latest technology and techniques to ensure your comfort and satisfaction.
