Our Story

BUILT BY ATHLETES, FOR ATHLETES.

Iron Athletics started in a 1,200 sq ft warehouse in 2018 with a barbell, some bumper plates, and a simple idea: build a gym where the training is hard, the coaching is real, and the community has your back.

Seven years later, we've grown into Pacific Beach's most serious training facility — but the energy hasn't changed. Walk through the door and you'll feel it.
